Velvet Venom

single-player fast-paced 2d top-down zombie shooter.

About

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move around.
  • Mouse to aim around.
  • Left Mouse Key to shoot using primary fire (machinegun).
  • Right Mouse Key to shoot using alt fire (shotgun).
  • R to suck blood pools, prioritizing hp.
  • F to suck blood pools, prioritizing blood (ammo).
  • LShift or Space to dash. (dashing grants you i-frames and slashes through enemies, stunning and disarming them and stealing their life for hp and blood).
  • F in menus to toggle fullscreen on/off.
  • ESC to pause/unpause the game.

Objective:

Exterminate all the zombies

Tips and Tricks

  • Don't stop moving.
  • Move in circular motion, especially with armed zombies around.
  • Spam dash.
  • Keep your distance from shooters, especially those with the shotguns.
  • Get close to giant zombies and alt-fire them.
  • Slash-dash through zombies to stun and disarm them.
  • Refill all your hp and blood after every encounter.
